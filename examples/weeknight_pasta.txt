User: Quick weeknight pasta idea?

Assistant: Aglio e olio. Boil spaghetti. While it cooks, gently warm thinly sliced garlic in olive oil with a pinch of chili flakes until fragrant but not browned. Toss the drained pasta in the oil with a splash of the starchy pasta water, then finish with chopped parsley and parmesan. Ten minutes, five ingredients.
